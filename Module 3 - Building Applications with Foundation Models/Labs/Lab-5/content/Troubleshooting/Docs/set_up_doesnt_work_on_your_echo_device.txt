Some things you can try when your Echo device has difficulty setting up.

Most problems with device setup arise from a few common issues. If they come up, try the following.

1. Check that your device has a strong internet connection.
2. If your device setup uses the Alexa app, check that you have the latest version of the app.
3. Restart your Echo device. Unplug it from power, wait 30 seconds, then plug it back in.
4. If none of these steps help, or if you have problems registering the device, reset your Echo device to clear up most issues.
